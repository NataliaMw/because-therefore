# Because, Therefore Demo Script

Target length: under 3 minutes.

## 0:00-0:15 — Open

Show the home screen.

Narration: “Because, Therefore is a multiplayer party game about causation. The joke is simple: history gives you chaos, and you have to rebuild the because-then-therefore chain.”

Click `Quick Play / Solo`.

## 0:15-0:35 — Scrambled Round

Show the Molasses Flood round with the scrambled evidence pile.

Narration: “Each round is a real event. These cards are all mixed up, and one of them is a trap: it sounds connected, but it did not cause the disaster.”

Click a few cards and place them into slots.

## 0:35-1:10 — Build The Chain

Arrange the Molasses Flood chain:

1. Wartime industrial alcohol demand
2. Rushed, poorly tested tank
3. Leaks and groans ignored
4. Warm delivery and temperature rise increase pressure
5. Tank ruptures
6. Molasses wave crushes the North End
7. Lawsuit and engineering rules

Leave the anarchist rumor card in the evidence pile.

Narration: “You score for exact order, for correct adjacencies, and for excluding the trap. So it rewards the logic between events, not just memorizing trivia.”

## 1:10-1:35 — Aha Reveal

Click `Submit Chain`.

Show the animated true chain snapping into place.

Narration: “The reveal is the aha-cascade. The game shows the true sequence and explains why each link is causal.”

Pause on the score and scoreboard.

## 1:35-2:00 — Trap Lesson

Zoom or pause on the trap lesson.

Narration: “The trap card teaches correlation versus causation. A rumor may be historically related, but it did not cause the tank to fail. The actual chain is engineering, pressure, rupture, flood.”

## 2:00-2:35 — GPT-5.6 Custom Round

Type a topic into `New round topic`, such as `The Suez Canal blockage`, and click `Generate Round`.

Narration: “With an API key, GPT-5.6 powers generateRound. It turns any topic into a fresh playable chain with strict JSON: title, blurb, ordered causal cards, explanations, and one plausible-but-wrong trap.”

If no API key is available, show the fallback label.

Narration: “Without a key, it falls back to built-in rounds, so the demo is still fully playable.”

## 2:35-2:55 — Close

Show the scoreboard and another round starting.

Narration: “Because, Therefore is built for parties, classrooms, and anyone who has ever mistaken ‘happened nearby’ for ‘therefore caused it.’ History is just a Rube Goldberg machine with paperwork.”
